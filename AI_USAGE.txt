Browsers enforce CORS to prevent malicious websites from reading data from another domain without permission.

Your request:

GET https://kultuuriaken.tartu.ee/sites/.../pipiannelinnas.jpg

Fails because the server does not send the header:

const express = require('express');

app.get('/proxy-image', async (req, res) => {

fetch('http://localhost:3000/proxy-image')
